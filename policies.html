Security

At Veil CRM, we implement comprehensive, industry-leading security measures to protect your data and ensure the integrity of our platform. Our multi-layered security approach includes:

Data Protection

Our end-to-end encryption system ensures that your sensitive data remains secure throughout its lifecycle:

• AES-256 encryption for data at rest
• TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
• Zero-knowledge encryption for sensitive user data
• Regular encryption key rotation and management

Blockchain Security

We maintain rigorous blockchain security standards:

• Quarterly smart contract audits by leading security firms
• Multi-signature wallet implementation for treasury management
• Automated vulnerability scanning of smart contracts
• Real-time monitoring of on-chain activities

Infrastructure Security

Our infrastructure is protected by multiple security layers:

• AWS Shield Advanced for DDoS protection
• Web Application Firewall (WAF) with custom rulesets
• Regular penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
• 24/7 security monitoring and incident response

Access Control

We implement strict access control measures:

• Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all user accounts
• Role-based access control (RBAC) for platform features
• Secure key management systems with hardware security modules
• Regular access review and privilege audit trails

Compliance and Certifications

Our security measures adhere to global standards:

• ISO 27001 certified security management
• SOC 2 Type II compliance
• GDPR and CCPA compliant data handling
• Regular third-party security assessments

We continuously update our security measures to address emerging threats and maintain the highest standards of data protection. For any security concerns or to report vulnerabilities, please contact our security team at security@veilcrm.com.

IPR Complaints

We take intellectual property rights seriously and have established comprehensive procedures for addressing IPR-related concerns:

• Complaint Filing: Submit detailed information about the alleged infringement to support@veilcrm.com, including proof of ownership and specific instances of violation

• Response Timeline: We aim to acknowledge all IPR complaints within 48 hours and provide initial assessment within 5 business days

• Investigation Process: Our legal team thoroughly investigates each complaint, gathering necessary evidence and consulting relevant parties

• Resolution Measures: We take appropriate action including content removal, account suspension, or other remedial measures as needed

• Appeals Process: Users can appeal decisions through our dedicated appeals channel within 30 days of resolution

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for willful intellectual property violations while ensuring fair evaluation of all claims.

Abuse Policy

Veil CRM maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy toward platform abuse to ensure a secure and trustworthy environment for all users. Our comprehensive abuse prevention and response framework includes:

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Unauthorized access attempts or system manipulation
  • Malicious smart contract deployment or interaction
  • Distribution of malware or harmful code
  • Impersonation or identity theft
  • Spam or automated abuse of platform features
  • Harassment or threatening behavior

Reporting Procedures

To report abuse:

  1. Document the incident with relevant details (time, date, nature of abuse)
  2. Gather supporting evidence (screenshots, transaction hashes)
  3. Submit a report to support@veilcrm.com or through our dashboard
  4. Include your contact information for follow-up

Response Protocol

Our security team follows a structured response protocol:

  • Immediate acknowledgment of abuse reports within 24 hours
  • Investigation and evidence verification
  • Implementation of necessary security measures
  • Communication with affected parties
  • Documentation and review of incidents

Violations may result in immediate account suspension, blacklisting of associated addresses, and potential legal action where applicable.
